Hi! My name is Yuefan Shen and I am a Ph.D. student focusing on CG(Computer Graphics) at State Key Lab of CAD&CG, Zhejiang University, advised by Youyi Zheng. I received my B.Eng. degree in Digital Media Technology from Shandong University in 2019. My research interests including 3D geometry processing and reconstruction with deep learning.

Previously, I worked closely with Yanchao Yang, who was a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at Stanford University. In 2022, I was a Research Scientist Intern at Meta Reality Labs, Pittsburgh, working with Giljoo Nam.

I am actively looking for a research position in 2024. Please feel free to contact me if you are interested in my research.
